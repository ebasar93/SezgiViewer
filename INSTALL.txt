SezgiViewer — Installation
==========================

1. Drag "SezgiViewer" onto the "Applications" folder in this window.

2. The first time you open it, macOS may say the app is "damaged" or that it
   "cannot be opened because the developer cannot be verified."
   This is expected — the app is free and simply not registered with Apple.

   To fix it (one time only):
     • Open the "Terminal" app (press Cmd+Space, type Terminal, hit Return)
     • Copy and paste this line, then press Return:

         xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/SezgiViewer.app

3. Now open SezgiViewer from your Applications folder (or Launchpad) normally.
   It will open without any warnings from now on.

That's it. Enjoy!

---------------------------------------------------------------
What it does: adds your PDF files, pulls out every highlight, and
exports them into one clean summary PDF. Add PDFs with the + button
(or drag them in), press Refresh, then Export.
